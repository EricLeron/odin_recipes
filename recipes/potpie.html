Chicken Pot Pie


A delicious family sized dish that will remind you of Sundays with your grandmother

This recipe does take a certain level of skill so don't get discouraged if it isn't perfect your first time around.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • butter, pre-chilled
  • chicken broth
  • frozen carrots and peas
  • chicken
  • onion
  • milk

Directions

  1. Roast your chicken in the oven until it reaches and internal temperature of 165. Set aside to cool
  2. Cut your cutter into your flour. Add water and roll your flour into two quarter inch thick circles. Circles should match the size of your pot.
  3. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees F
  4. Make a butter and flour roux and add hot chicken broth to it
  5. Add your milk to the "gravy" to give it a creamier texture
  6. Add your chicken, chopped onions, and frozen vegetables to your gravy
  7. Add the pot pie mixture to your crust and cover
  8. Cook for 45 minutes. Cover the top with aluminum foil if it starts to get too dark
  9. Wait for pie to cool and enjoy
