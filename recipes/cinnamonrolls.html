Easy Cinnamon Rolls


A quick and delicious snack that can be enjoyed any time.

The recipe shown here is a nice hack recipe that doesn't require you to make anything from scratch

Ingredients

  • Pillsbury Cinnamon Rolls
  • Butter
  • Heavy Whipping Cream

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 F
  2. Use butter to grease your baking dish and add your cinnamon rolls
  3. Cover your rolls with aluminum foil (optional)
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
  5. Let cool and enjoy
