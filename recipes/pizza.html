Detroit Style Pizza


A recipe for one of the lesser appreciated styles of pizza.

A Detroit Style pizza is known for it's fluffy crust and crispy cheese edges. The minimal amount of time necessary to prepare to recipe is 4 hours, so you will need to plan ahead.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasonings
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • Cheese of choice
  • Toppings of choice

Directions

  1. Add you yeast and sugar to warm water to ensure that yeast is still active
  2. Mix your flour and salt together and add water yeast mixture. There should be no dry flour remaining
  3. Let rest for 15-25 minutes
  4. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes. You should feel changes in the dough over time
  5. Add needed dough to a greased bowl, cover and allow to rise at room temperature for 1.5 hours
  6. Stretch dough out to you desired deep pan and allow to rise for 1 hour
  7. While the dough is rising, prepare your cheese(if necessary) and tomato sauce. Also preheat your oven to 425 degress F
  8. For tomato sauce, add chopped garlic to hot pan containing olive oil
  9. Allow Garlic to cook for a little while but to long
  10. Add your Italian seasonings and salt to taste
  11. Cook your dough in the over for approx. 12 minutes. Dough should be done at this point
  12. Add your sauce and cheese, and toppings to your pizza. MAke sure to add cheese all the way to the edge of the pan to get the signature crispy-cheesy edges
  13. Cook under the broiler until you are satisfied with the cheese.
